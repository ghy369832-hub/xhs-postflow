# Health Content Safety

Use this for health, medical, nutrition, fitness, supplements, psychology, or disease-related posts.

## Hard Rules

- Do not diagnose.
- Do not promise cure, reversal, prevention, or treatment effect.
- Avoid absolute food rules such as “一定要吃”, “千万不能吃”, “吃了就好”, “不吃就没事”.
- Prefer softer wording: “可以优先考虑”, “少量/适量”, “如果医生有特殊要求按医嘱”, “不适合所有人”.
- Add a brief disclaimer when relevant: symptoms, medication, pregnancy, chronic disease, or abnormal test results should consult a professional.
- Do not invent study results, clinical numbers, or authority endorsements.

## Safer Reframes

- “3吃3不吃” -> “甲状腺不舒服时，饮食上可以先留意这 3 类”
- “甲减必吃” -> “甲减人群常被提醒关注的营养点”
- “结节不能碰” -> “如果医生要求控制，先问清楚这类食物是否适合你”

The final draft can still be catchy, but it must not trade accuracy for clicks.
